Dear Professor

I have redefined my research subject after the last discussion:
How and What should the Youth learn in the digital media environment.

The following is could be the initial structure and main content of my thesis. I maybe will refine it according to the reading and research that I find.

  1. Introduction

  2. Skills
    In this section I will talk about What we should learn before we learn in the digital media world, I mainly refer to 21st century skills and Henry Jenkins’s Confronting the Challenges of Participatory Culture: Media Education for the 21st Century

  3. Products
    In this part I try to analyze the existing forms of digital learning products e.g. Mooc, Connected learning and so forth.

  4. Youth
    I borrowed the definition of Youth from the book of Henry Jenkins By Any Media Necessary - The New Youth Activism.

    In this book, the term “youth” refers to people in their teens or twenties. It defines not simply a stage of physiological or psychological development, but also a stage in the process of acquiring the skills necessary for political participation at an age where there is less than complete access to the rights of citizenship.

    Therefore in this section I try to research what do the Youth need/behavior during the processing of learning.

  5. Participations
    It seems that no matter what form of digital learning we can not avoid not talking about Participation. So this part will be around the engagement of youth.

  6. Case Study

  7. Project
